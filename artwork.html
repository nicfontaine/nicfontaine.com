Burrito Project SF Logo

¶

Burrito Project SF is a nonprofit group that prepares and distributes hundreds of vegan burritos, monthly or biweekly, to shelters and amongst the ever-evolving landscape of people living on the street; or just in need, in San Francisco.

As part of the organizational team, I currently run distribution for deliveries, volunteer signups, and deliver as well. I was asked to design the new logo, and was excited to refresh the look as well as give some of my own style. I made sure to highlight notable city landmarks, the simple & self-subsistent delivery method of a bike, and a stack of fat burritos. It's effectively casual, communicative, and recognizable -- and something I'd wear on a shirt at the end of the day.

Bunker Blast

¶

A contract project for The Littlest Golfer, Bunker Blast incorporates the original characters Sandy, Putter, Steve, and Stinky into a 2D brick-breaker/Peggle style Unity game. The game tours references to world-famous courses in its four chapters, and has plenty of content with 40 total levels.

I created UI, environment textures, character animation sprites, puzzle-based levels, promo materials, logos, and icons.

Honestly, for a kids game it's quite challenging, and the final level is pretty ridiculous.
